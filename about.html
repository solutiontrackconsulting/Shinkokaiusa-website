About Us

私たちについて

We help families navigate one of the most significant transitions in a senior's life — returning home to Japan.

MISSION
ミッション

Every person deserves to spend their later years in a place that feels like home. For many Japanese seniors living in the United States, that means returning to Japan — to family, familiar culture, and a care system built around them.

「すべての人が、故郷と感じられる場所で晩年を過ごす権利があります。アメリカに暮らす多くの日本人高齢者にとって、それは日本への帰国を意味します。」

APPROACH
アプローチ

We work closely with seniors and their adult children to understand the full picture — care needs, family circumstances, and personal wishes — and provide guidance on the options available in Japan. We help families make informed decisions and navigate the process with confidence.

「私たちは、高齢者本人やそのご家族と緊密に連携し、ケアニーズ、家族の状況、ご本人の希望を丁寧に把握した上で、日本での選択肢についてアドバイスします。」

OUR PARENT COMPANY
親会社について

Shinkokai USA is a subsidiary of Shinkoufukushikai, a trusted Japanese social welfare organization with decades of experience dedicated to the dignity and wellbeing of seniors. Our work in the United States is a direct extension of their mission.

「シンコウカイUSAは、高齢者の尊厳と福祉に長年尽力してきた伸こう福祉会の子会社です。私たちの米国での活動は、伸こう福祉会のミッションの直接的な延長です。」
